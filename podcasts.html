The Tech Bubble Podcast

LISTEN: The Tech Bubble on Spotify

Role: Lead Editor


For the better part of 2020, this project was a huge part of my life. I made it my CAS project. It was my escape, a brief respite of calm during the storm that was the IB. During my run I edited a total of 8 episodes, seeing the podcast from its early stages and the initial trial run through to the last episode made in 2020 (and my last!), fittingly a 2020 retrospective. In 2020 alone it has seen SIS students, teachers, alumni, principals, tech companies, even the CEO of ESF Belinda Greer (!) debate key topics, be those issues or advances, in technology. I was fortunate to meet and work alongside a cast of amazing people; the podcast would not be where it is now without the contributions of everybody in the team, and I am extremely grateful to have had this opportunity.
